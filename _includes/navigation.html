Navigation

The navigation pane will show you your current spot on the map as you are driving, in real-time. The map gives you three options:

  • Free form (drag/pan and zoom)
  • Fixed on truck, up is always north
  • Fixed on truck, map rotates so up is direction truck is facing

Additionally, the following information is available:

  • Speed limit
  • Estimated time of arrival
  • Distance remaining
  • Time remaining until arrival
